# PositioningHQ

**Build your company's complete Positioning Playbook in a day — using AI as your strategic partner.**

---

Most companies have a positioning problem. Every rep tells a slightly different story. Marketing says one thing. Sales says another. The website says a third. Buyers notice — and it costs you deals you should be winning.

PositioningHQ is a structured workshop and toolkit that fixes this. In a half-day working session, your leadership team builds a complete, company-specific Positioning Playbook, competitive battlecards, and a call prep tool — all live, all working, all yours to keep.

---

## What you walk away with

**A complete Positioning Playbook** — seven structured sections covering your market, your ideal customer, your three core value drivers, your competitive moat, and your positioning statement. The internal source of truth your whole team works from.

**Competitive battlecards** — one card per key competitor. How they position, where they're weak, and exactly how to win against them in a deal. Ready to hand to your reps on Day 1.

**A call prep tool** — before any significant sales conversation, your reps run a skill that generates a personalized brief: opening hypothesis, discovery questions, proof points, and a recommended next step. Matched to the prospect and the stage of the deal.

**A working AI system** — your team leaves knowing how to use Claude as a professional tool. The vault is set up, the skills are installed, and the system compounds over time.

## The framework

The Positioning Playbook is built on seven elements, structured around a single insight: your solution only makes sense inside your customer's context. Most frameworks start with the product. This one ends there.

| #   | Element                          | What it defines                                                                                         |
| --- | -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 01  | Market Overview — The "Why Now?" | The market shifts and windows of opportunity that make your solution necessary right now                |
| 02  | Target Customers & ICP           | The demographic and characteristics of your right-fit customers — who you win with, reliably            |
| 03  | Value Drivers                    | The three outcomes your ideal customers find genuinely valuable — what they'll shift budget to achieve  |
| 04  | Distinct Advantages (Moat)       | How you do it differently and better than anyone else for your target customers                         |
| 05  | Competitive Intelligence         | Deep, up-to-date knowledge of your competitors — how they position, where they're weak, how to win      |
| 06  | Positioning Your Solution        | How your solution is a leader at delivering something a well-defined set of customers cares a lot about |
| 07  | Proof Points                     | The evidence behind your claims — case studies, testimonials, analyst reports, win stories              |

*Framework informed by the work of April Dunford.*

## The six skills

Once installed, these skills are available in Claude Desktop:

| Skill | What it does |
|---|---|
| `/setup-positioning-vault` | ⭐ **Start here.** One-time setup — creates your company vault, collects your context, and automatically runs research and playbook generation |
| `/company-research` | 9-step market and competitive analysis: who you are, who your customers are, where you win, and where competitors are vulnerable |
| `/positioning-playbook` | Builds the full 7-section Positioning Playbook from your research — your living source of truth |
| `/battlecard` | Generates one battlecard per competitor — how to position, how to win, what questions to ask |
| `/call-plan` | Personalized pre-call brief for any deal, any stage — opening hypothesis, discovery questions, proof points, next step |
| `/product-launch` | Full launch plan via a structured interview — positioning impact, messaging, and rollout |

The typical flow:

```
/setup-positioning-vault
        ↓  (automatically runs research + playbook)
/battlecard   /call-plan   /product-launch
```
