3. **Cost of Inaction:** A single AI-related data incident — an employee feeding sensitive data into a public AI model, a hallucinated output used in a client deliverable — can trigger regulatory scrutiny, client contract review, and reputational damage that outweighs years of advisory fees.

6. **Proof Points:** ISO 42001 is the first international AI management system standard — formally published in 2023 and rapidly becoming a procurement requirement in enterprise supply chains.
