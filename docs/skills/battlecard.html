Competitive
Battlecards

One markdown battlecard per competitor — built for sales new hire enablement. Auto-loads your positioning playbook and knowledge base from memory, then enriches with live web research.

$ /battlecard

Workflow

1

Identify Competitors

Extract from your inputs or ask: "Which competitors should I build battlecards for?"

2

Load Internal Knowledge

Auto-reads your positioning playbook from /memory/positioning-playbook/ and all files from /memory/knowledgebase/.

3

Research Each Competitor

Web searches: positioning, head-to-head comparisons, pricing, customer logos, and recent news — filling gaps not covered by your internal materials.

4

Generate One Card Per Competitor

Uses the battlecard template to produce a consistent, scannable markdown file per competitor.

5

Confidence Summary

After all cards are generated, outputs a confidence rating (High / Medium / Low) for each and lists any remaining gaps.

What's In Each Battlecard

Their PitchHow they describe themselves and what they lead with.
StrengthsWhat they genuinely do well — be honest.
WeaknessesKnown gaps, complaints, pricing friction points.
Landmine QuestionsDiscovery questions that expose their weakness naturally.
Our DifferentiatorsSpecific reasons to choose your product over theirs.
Proof PointsCustomer wins, outcomes, and case study references.
Objection HandlingWhen a prospect says "but [competitor] has X..."
When We Win / LoseDeal signal patterns from real win/loss data.
