Company Research

9-step structured research workflow. Give it a company name and website — get back a complete market intelligence document covering product, market, customers, competition, and synthesis.

$ /company-research Stripe | stripe.com

Research Steps

The skill runs 9 sequential steps, reading detailed instructions from assets/ for each. All enriched with live web search.

1

Company Overview

Founding history, business model, revenue, team size, funding status.

2

Product / Service Analysis

Core product lines, pricing model, key features, technical differentiation.

3

Market Segment

TAM/SAM, category definition, market positioning, growth trends.

4

Customer Segment

ICP, buyer personas, use cases, customer logos, and industry verticals.

5

Strengths

Sustainable competitive advantages, moats, brand, partnerships.

6

Weaknesses

Known gaps, complaints, pricing concerns, coverage limits.

7

Recent Developments

Product launches, funding rounds, leadership changes, press coverage.

8

Competitive Analysis

Head-to-head comparison matrix against key competitors.

9

Final Synthesis

Executive summary combining all findings into a positioning-ready document.
