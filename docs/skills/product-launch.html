Product Launch
Planner

A 4-phase launch planning skill: founder interview, strategy checkpoint, 6 output files, and a positioning impact assessment that rates your launch across 5 strategic dimensions.

$/product-launch

Four Phases

1

Founder Interview

Conversational interview across 5 blocks: product, customer, market, launch context, and channels. 15–20 exchanges with follow-up probing on thin answers.

2

Strategy Checkpoint

Summarises understanding back to the founder before generating anything. Must be confirmed before output generation begins.

3

Generate 6 Output Files

Pass 1 (strategy layer) first — messaging narrative, GTM plan, success metrics. Founder reviews before Pass 2 (execution layer) begins.

4

Positioning Impact Assessment

Rates the launch across 5 dimensions, assigns an overall impact level (0–3), and generates a positioning update recommendation file if warranted.

6 Output Files

Pass 1 — Strategy
launch-messaging-narrative.md
Core message, value prop, audience, and tone-of-voice for the launch.
Pass 1 — Strategy
launch-gtm-channel-plan.md
Channel-by-channel plan grounded in founder's actual assets and budget.
Pass 1 — Strategy
launch-success-metrics.md
30-day and 90-day KPIs, leading indicators, and tracking approach.
Pass 2 — Execution
launch-sales-enablement-brief.md
What sales needs to know: pitch, objections, competitive context.
Pass 2 — Execution
launch-internal-faq.md
Internal Q&A for your team to speak confidently about the launch.
Pass 2 — Execution
launch-announcement.md
Ready-to-publish launch announcement for blog, email, or social.

Phase 4 — Positioning Impact Assessment

Evaluates the launch across 5 dimensions and assigns a change rating (None / Partial / Full) for each. Overall impact level determines what gets generated.

Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)None · Partial · Full
Market SegmentNone · Partial · Full
Positioning & CategoryNone · Partial · Full
Value Drivers & Key MessagesNone · Partial · Full
Differentiation & Brand StoryNone · Partial · Full

Impact levels: Level 0 = no file. Level 1–3 = positioning-update-recommendation.md generated, scaling in depth from targeted additions to a full rewrite blueprint.
