📞 SalesCall Prep

Sales Call Prep

Personalized, stage-specific call plans output as .docx. Always researches the prospect company first. Every question tied to your positioning playbook.

$/call-plan

Four Call Stages

discovery

Discovery

First call. Hypothesis-driven questions tied to known pains from your positioning.

demo

Demo

Use-case focused walkthrough tied to what was uncovered in discovery.

follow-up

Follow-Up

Multi-stakeholder progression, new objections, expanding the champion.

closing

Closing

Commercial negotiation, final objections, and concrete next-step ask.

Workflow

1

Collect Inputs

Positioning playbook, prospect name/role, company, and call stage. Optional: tech stack, prior notes.

2

Research Prospect Company

Always web-searches: overview, recent news, leadership, tech stack — to personalise the opening hypothesis.

3

Load Call Structure

Reads references/call-structures.md for the precise structure for the chosen stage.

4

Generate .docx

Complete call plan with no placeholder text. Research gaps flagged explicitly. Output is Word-ready.

Output

File format.docx (Word document)
File namecall-plan-[prospect]-[stage]-[date].docx
Chat summary2–3 sentence brief + research gaps
Web searchYes — live prospect research always runs
