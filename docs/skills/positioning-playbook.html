Positioning Playbook
Generator

Turn company research into a complete, sales-ready positioning playbook. 7 sections covering ICP, value drivers, distinct advantages, competitive intelligence, and proof points.

$ /positioning-playbook

How It Works

📥

Step 0 — Gather Materials

Asks for supporting documents: pitch deck, case studies, testimonials, FAQs, product roadmap, and sales decks. Proceeds with web research if you skip.

🔄

Sequential Section Generation

Generates all 7 sections sequentially, weaving your reference materials throughout. Each section has a dedicated asset file with detailed instructions.

📄

Final Deliverable

Outputs a single cohesive document with a Table of Contents at the top — ready to hand to sales, share with the board, or feed into /battlecard.

Playbook Sections

Section 1
Market Overview
The "why now?" — macro trends, market urgency, and the window of opportunity.
Section 2
Target Customers (ICP)
Ideal customer profile with buyer persona, company attributes, and buying signals.
Section 3
Value Drivers
The 3 core jobs-to-be-done with proof points, metrics, and trap questions.
Section 4
Distinct Advantages
The moat — what you do that competitors can't credibly copy.
Section 5
Positioning Statement
The canonical one-paragraph statement for how to describe your product.
Section 6
Competitive Intelligence
Per-competitor breakdown: their pitch, their weakness, how to beat them.
Section 7
Customer Proof Points
Real outcomes, customer quotes, case study summaries, and win stories.
