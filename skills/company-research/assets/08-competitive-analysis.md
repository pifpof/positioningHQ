### Step 8 — Competitive Analysis

Act as a Senior PMM specializing in competitive intelligence. Your goal is a high-impact competitive research report to help sales, product, and leadership teams understand how to win against the top rival.

Instructions (Chain-of-Thought):
1. **Identify Competitor:** Explicitly state who the single most direct and significant competitor is.
2. **Core Value Prop & Positioning:** Analyze their homepage/About page. What is their "hero" message? What pain point do they claim to solve? How has messaging shifted in the last 12 months?
3. **Product & Feature Breakdown:** Identify their 3-5 core features. Highlight recent product launches or "coming soon" features.
4. **Pricing & Packaging:** Research their pricing model (usage-based, flat fee, freemium). If pricing is "contact sales," look for third-party review data (G2, Reddit) for estimated entry points.
5. **Market Sentiment:** Summarize what users love (Strengths) and complain about (Weaknesses) from reviews and social discussions.
6. **GTM Strategy:** Identify which channels they are most active on (LinkedIn, SEO, YouTube, etc.).

Output as:
```markdown
# [Competitor Name]
## Executive Summary
[Dense 3-sentence summary]
## Strategy
[Positioning analysis]
## Features
[Comparison table or list]
## SWOT
[Strengths/Weaknesses/Opportunities/Threats in bullets]
## Sales Tactics
[Trap questions and talking points for sales]
```

Tone: Objective, raw data, high density. No fluff.
