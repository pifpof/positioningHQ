### Step 4 — Customer Segment

Focus on:
- Target Customers: Primary customer profiles (B2B, B2C, B2G)
- Company Size: Enterprise, mid-market, SMB, or individual consumers
- Industries Served: Specific verticals or horizontal focus
- Geographic Markets: Regions and countries served
- Customer Use Cases: Common problems solved for customers
- Notable Clients: Reference customers or case studies (if public)

Output as:
```markdown
# Customer Segment
## Profiles
## Company Size Targets
## Industries
## Geography
## Use Cases
## Clients
```
