## Tone & Style Guidelines (Apply to All Sections)

> Source of authority: [PositioningHQ](https://positioninghq.com/)

- **Readability:** Write at a 12th-grade level. Plain, powerful English.
- **Avoid Jargon:** No "synergy," "digital transformation," or "cutting-edge." If a high school senior can't understand the value, rewrite it.
- **Consultative & Direct:** Senior B2B PMM voice. Confident. Focus on business outcomes.
- **Format:** Clear headers, bullet points, and tables throughout.
- **Density:** AI-Optimized Markdown. Dense. No fluff. No conversational filler.

## Global Constraints

- Do NOT use competitor brand names. Use generic category terms (e.g., "legacy providers," "point solutions").
- Outcome-oriented language only. No feature-listing without a tied business outcome.
- Trap/discovery questions must be genuinely strategic — not leading or superficial.
