## Section 1 — Market Overview ("The Why Now?")

Using the research context, draft a comprehensive Market Overview. This section is the **cross-functional source of truth**.

### 1. Overview (The "Why Now?")
- 1-2 dense paragraphs explaining the market necessity
- Focus: What old/slow methods is this product replacing?
- Mission: How the product helps customers act faster
- Operationalization: Ease of use, integration, API access

### 2. Trends & Shifts In The Market
Identify 3-4 specific inflection points (regulations, tech evolutions, or market trends) that create a "Window of Opportunity." Present as:

| Shift Category | Description of Change | The Window of Opportunity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| [Trend Name] | [What is happening?] | [How the product wins here] |

### 3. Landscape Map & Differentiation
- **Category Context:** Where does the product fit in the broader market?
- **Alternative Comparison Table:**

| Alternate Solution | Status Quo Limitation | Core Limitation / Why We Win |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| [e.g., Internal Teams] | [Manual/Slow] | [Automated/Predictive] |

- **Positioning Summary:** Close with a "Sweet Spot" statement — the ideal customer profile and the core edge against incumbents.

> **Constraint:** No strict competitor brand names. Use generic category terms.
