## Section 5 — Positioning Statement

This is the **internal source of truth** that aligns Product, Marketing, and Sales on differentiated value.

### 1. Internal Positioning Statement

| Field | Positioning Component |
| :--- | :--- |
| **For** | [Target ICP / Statement of need] |
| **Who are currently dissatisfied with** | [Alternate Solutions or Status Quo] |
| **Our Product is a** | [Product Category or Market] |
| **That provides** | [Key Benefit / Primary Reason to Buy] |
| **Unlike** | [Primary Competitive Alternative] |
| **We** | [The Single Most Critical Differentiator] |

### 2. Message Variations

- **Sales Enablement Variation:** A 1-2 paragraph elevator pitch for reps. Lead with value and address the specific problem-solution fit.
- **Investor/Executive Variation:** A concise strategic summary focused on market constraints, the "Why Now," and the scalable advantage.
- **Website Copy Variation:** A customer-facing headline and sub-headline (H1/H2) that translates technical advantages into human benefits.

> **Constraint:** Short, clear, logic-based. Internal strategy aligned.
