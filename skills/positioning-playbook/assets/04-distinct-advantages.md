## Section 4 — Distinct Advantages (The Moat)

Identify 2-3 deep, unique capabilities that competitors cannot easily replicate. For each moat:

### 1. Description of the Advantage
- **What it is:** A specific, proprietary capability (unique data source, specialized algorithm, integration workflow)
- **The "Unfair" Element:** Why is this hard for a competitor or legacy incumbent to copy?

### 2. Why Customers Care (The "So What?")
- **Value Translation:** Move beyond the feature — what is the specific business impact on the customer's daily life or bottom line?
- **The Strategic Shift:** How does this move them from a "Lagging" state to a "Leading/Predictive" state?

### 3. Proof Points (Evidence)
- 2-3 specific ways to prove this advantage is real (metrics, data volumes, proprietary methodologies)

### 4. Discovery Prompts (The "Trap" Questions)
- 2-3 questions a sales rep can use to highlight a competitor's weakness in this area **without naming them**
- Objective: Lead the customer to realize they have a gap that only this product can fill

> **Constraint:** 2-3 deep moats beats 5 shallow features. No competitor brand names.
